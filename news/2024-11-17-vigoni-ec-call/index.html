Call for Applications: Villa Vigoni-Gespraech on Europe and the Geoeconomic Challenge

November 2024 · Timur Ergen

Together with Donato Di Carlo, Anke Hassel, Kate McNamara, and Manuela Moschella I’m organizing the Villa Vigoni-Gespräch ‘Europe and the Geoeconomic Challenge’, 22 to 24 April 2025, Loveno di Menaggio, Italy. We’re inviting applications for up to three advanced students and early career researchers to participate. The deadline for applications is 10 January 2025.

A detailed call for applications can be found here.
